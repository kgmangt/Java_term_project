My Final Term Project: Weapon Enhancement Game
To be honest, my Java skills are still a work in progress, but I wanted to challenge myself to build a game from scratch. This project is a final requirement for my course, but I believe it will be a valuable stepping stone for my future as a developer. I know there will be plenty of hurdles along the way, but I’m excited to see it through to the end!

🎮 Game Explanation
The concept is simple: Enhance your weapons, sell them, and stack your profits!

The Risks: The higher the enhancement level, the lower your success rate. If you fail? Your weapon will vanish from your inventory!

The Rewards: Use the money you’ve earned from your successful enhancements to buy better items and boost your success rate.

The Goal: Keep pushing your luck until you forge the ultimate, "shiny" weapon at level 30!
